Welcome to my Final Project for CSC 106

In our action-packed gaming program, players face the challenge of eliminating waves of descending zombies to rack up points.
With a limit of three lives, strategic decisions are crucial to survival. The escalating difficulty and relentless undead onslaught create
an intense, immersive experience, complemented by a seamlessly integrated narrative that adds depth to the game.
Adding a unique twist, the game concludes if three zombies pass the screen unscathed, prompting players to restart and refine their strategies.
This dynamic cycle, coupled with the thrill of surpassing previous scores, ensures a captivating and engaging gaming experience.
